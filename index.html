💻 PC Installation (Chrome/Brave/Edge)

1
Download and extract the .zip folder.
2
Go to chrome://extensions in your browser.
3
Turn on "Developer Mode" (Top Right).
4
Click "Load Unpacked" and select the folder.

📱 Mobile Installation (Android Only)

1
Install Kiwi Browser from the Play Store.
2
Download the Matcha .zip to your phone.
3
In Kiwi, tap the 3 dots > Extensions.
4
Enable Developer Mode.
5
Tap "+ (from .zip)" and select the Matcha file.
